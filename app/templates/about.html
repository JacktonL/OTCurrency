About OT Currency

What is OT Currency?

OT Currency is a project made for Oakland Tech's 2019 Capstone class by Clemente Ferrer. It was designed using the Stanford d.school method of design for future generations of students at Oakland Tech. It is a website for promoting prosocial behavior in the community be enabling peers to reward each other for their actions. It also incentivizes such behavior by giving rewards to both the recipients and givers of currency. This currency can then be redeemed for prizes (theoretically when it is put into action within the school).

What is Currency (Your Wallet)?

Against all logic, currency on this website is invaluable. You get 10 currency per week and can give it to whoever you want for whatever reason you want. Both the sender and receiver get an equal amount of reputation for a transaction.

What is Reputation?

You get one reputation for every currency you send or recieve. If you send your friend 10 currency, you both get 10 reputation. Reputation has real value and can be redeemed.

In other words, reputation = money given + money received
